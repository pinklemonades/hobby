FASHION TIPS

1. Invest in Basics, Style with Personality

A plain white tee, high-waisted jeans, and a good blazer can go a long way. The secret? It’s how you style them. Add a bold necklace, a pop of color with your bag, or layer with confidence. Basics are your canvas - your vibe is the paint.

2. Accessories Change Everything

ven the simplest outfit can turn into a lewk with the right accessories. Statement earrings, cute sunnies, a mini handbag, or even a belt can add instant polish. Don’t be afraid to mix metals or go big - you’re the main character!

3. Fit Over Brand – Always

It doesn’t matter if it’s designer or thrifted - if it fits you perfectly, you’ll look expensive. Choose cuts that flatter your body shape, get pieces tailored if needed, and wear it with confidence. Confidence is the real luxury.
